About Me.

Hi, my name is Sang Yoon Byun, and I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science at Grinnell College (will graduate in May 2022). As an international student from South Korea, I came to the United States with a clear purpose in mind—to surround myself with creative people and ardently learn from them. In the near future, I aspire to be not merely a skilled software developer but also an innovator who creates a positive and lasting social impact in our society.

Since I first started programming, I have had a profound interest in artificial intelligence (AI), especially in the intersection of AI and computer vision. I have recently taken an undergraduate course on AI, covering a wide range of fundamental topics like Adversarial search, Bayesian learning, Markov chains, and Reinforcement learning. To further continue my study on AI, I have worked (and still continue to work) on various personal projects, which are listed below.

Technical Skills

  • Python, C, C++, Java, Scheme, HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • OpenCV, TensorFlow, Pandas/Numpy/Matplotlib
  • Windows and UNIX/Linux environments
  • MPI, Kokkos, OpenMP, CUDA
