About Me.

Hi, my name is Sang Yoon Byun, and I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science at Grinnell College (will graduate in May 2022). As an international student from South Korea, I came to the United States with a clear purpose in mind—to push myself out of the comfort zone and constantly surround myself with passionate and creative developers as well as researchers. In the near future, I aspire to be not merely a skilled software developer but also an innovator who can create a positive and lasting social impact in our society.

Since I first started programming, I have had a profound interest in artificial intelligence (AI), especially in the intersection of AI and computer vision. I have recently taken an undergraduate course on AI, covering a wide range of fundamental topics like Adversarial search, Bayesian learning, Markov chains, and Reinforcement learning. To further continue my study on AI, I have worked (and still continue to work) on various personal projects, which are listed below.

Projects.

Retinal OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) Classification

Constructed a CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) model and trained it on over 83,000 retinal OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) images to classify for macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy. The trained model was able to achieve over 90% accuracy rate on the test dataset.

Personal Facial Recognition

A facial recognition project with three main programs: (1) a face-capture program that takes a screenshot on every fifth frame and saves them as 128x128 images; (2) a face-trainging program that trains a CNN model to recognize a specific human face; and (3) a face-recognition program that uses the trained and frozen model to accurately detect and label different human faces.

  • #python
  • #facial_recognition
  • #deep_learning
  • #computer_vision

Lane Detection for Autonomous Vehicles

An enhanced lane detection system using computer vision techniques for autonomous vehicles. The program accurately detects and highlights lanes for different light conditions (shadows, unclear markings, etc). Additionally, it calculates and displays curvature and offset information for the vehicle at every frame.

  • #autonomous_vehicles

Face Filter

Detects human faces real-time in a webcam feed and applies up to ten (or more) different snapchat-like filters using the SSD (Single Shot MultiBox Detector) based neural network. The face filters are adaptive in that they are able to estimate the size of the faces and remain proportional and natural when a user moves closer or farther from the camera.

  • #single_shot_multibox_detector
  • #computer_vision

Pneumonia Classification

Built and customized a CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) model and trained it on over 5,200 patient chest x-ray images to detect signs of viral and bacterial pneumonia. The trained model was able to achieve over 89% accuracy rate on the test dataset.

Handmotion Control

Uses dense optical-flow (Farneback) to allow users to actively interact with a given camera and control the brightness and/or the contrast values of each video frame in real time. Using their hands, users can increase (wave up) or decrease (wave down) the contrast value and also increase (wave right) or decrease (wave left) the brightness value.

  • #opencv
  • #dense_optical_flow

Business Card Text Scanner

Uses edge detection and perspective warping methods to take a photo, automatically or manually warp the image, and interpret the text within the image. While the program's primary purpose is to scan business-cards of standard size (3.5 x 2 inches), the scanning code can easily be altered to be used in many other ways.

Digit Recognition

Accurately recognizes a handwritten digit (0-9) by a user on the screen provided. It uses HOG (Histogram of Oriented Gradients) feature descriptors and SVM (Support Vector Machine) model to identify certain features of a digit and determine the digit based on the SVM-trained data. A user can write on the screen with a cursor and press Space bar to submit to the program.

  • #HOG
  • #SVM
  • #computer_vision
